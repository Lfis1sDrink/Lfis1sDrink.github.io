Markdown Content:
据猫眼专业版最新数据，截至2月14日17时40分，导演饺子已凭借《哪吒》系列电影拿下153.09亿元票房成绩，超过徐克、张艺谋，成为中国导演票房榜第二。

Director Jiaozi has raked in a whopping 15.309 billion yuan in box office revenue with the Ne Zha film series as of Friday afternoon, surpassing Hark Tsui and Zhang Yimou, and ranking as the second-highest-grossing Chinese director at the box office, according to data from ticketing platform Maoyan.

同样冲上热搜的还有不断刷新的票房数据。14日下午，《哪吒2》官方微博发布了101亿和102亿的报喜海报。

自票房破1亿元开始，除了整数破亿海报外，饺子导演每破一亿就画一张，网友表示：大概导演都没想到要一直画下去……

Since the film’s box office surpassed 100 million yuan, director Jiaozi has been creating a celebratory comic illustration for every additional 100 million earned, in addition to the milestone posters. As of Thursday morning, he had drawn a total of 89 illustrations. Currently, the illustrations for the 10.3 billion and 10.4 billion yuan milestones are still in the works.

这些手绘海报，连起来看简直就是一部《哪吒》番外，影片中各个角色也都通过海报展现出了逗趣的一面。
